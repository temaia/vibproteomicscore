Frequently asked questions


Data analysis
What is LFQ?

LFQ (LabelFree Quantification) intensity values are the normalized MS intensity across all samples. The normalization corrects for differences in total protein amounts between the samples. This makes it possible to compare the samples. The LFQ intensity values are the values that you should use for further analysis.

What is iBAQ?

The iBAQ ratio is the MS intensity corrected for the theoretical detectability of the protein. The higher the iBAQ ratio, the more abundant a protein is WITHIN a sample.
