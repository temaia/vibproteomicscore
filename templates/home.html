Welcome to the VIB Proteomics Core portal!

This website is a an extension of our facility's main page and serves 2 goals:

  • As a registration platform for your proteomics project
  • As an access point to follow the progress of your experiment

If you want your samples to be analyzed by the VIB Proteomics core, please use the workflow below.

1. Contact the facility at an early stage and announce delivery

Before sending samples to the Facility, contact us in an early stage so your specific question can be discussed.

2. Register your project

When the project is accepted, you will receive a login to access the sample registration tab .

There you have to complete the form with all the information about the experiment youâ€™re about to send.

Note: Please try to fill in all the fields. That way, we can understand the goals and expectations of your experiment.

3. Send your sample according to the sample delivery guidelines

After project registration, arrangements on delivery date can be made with the Proteomics Core. All instructions can be found here.

4. Follow your project

During sample processing we will update the status of your project. This can be followed in the project status tab.

5. Check the results

The results of the analysis will be send to you from the moment the data-analysis is finished. If you have quenstions, you can always check our frequentlty asked questions tab. Don't hesitate to contact us if your questions are not solved.
